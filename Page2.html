Marauder Ranger Witch
The Marauder is a formidable warrior hailing from the Eternals, relying on raw strength and endurance. Equipped with a massive mace or axe, the Marauder excels in close combat, delivering devastating blows to foes and soaking up damage with thick armor. For those who seek finesse and agility. The Ranger is an ideal choice. A skilled archer with a natural connection to the wilderness, the Ranger can rain down arrows from a distance, taking down enemies swiftly and gracefully. The Witch, a master of dark arts, harnesses the power of elemental magic to obliterate her foes. Manipulating fire, ice, and lightning, she is a force to be reckoned with, decimating enemies from afar and controlling the battlefield with her spells.
Shadow Duelist Templar
The Shadow, a master of deception and trickery, is a mysterious and agile class in Path of Exile. Hailing from the forsaken town of Oriath, the Shadow is adept at using both daggers and traps, making him a formidable foe on the battlefield. The Duelist is a versatile swordsman who combines swiftness with strength, striking enemies with lethal precision. His mastery of both melee and ranged weaponry grants him the adaptability to overcome various challenges. The Templar serves as a righteous warrior and cleric, wielding a combination of martial prowess and divine magic. With a shield and mace in hand, he defends allies while smiting the wicked with divine retribution.
